About Me

Hi! I’m Michael Sekol, an experienced software development instructor and advocate for computer science education. My journey began in tech out of necessity, building servers and websites during my career in the mortgage industry. Since 2017, I’ve been teaching at Mahoning County Career and Technical Center, where I inspire students to unlock the doors to exciting tech careers.
